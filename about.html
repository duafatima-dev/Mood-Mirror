About Us

Mood Mirror is a simple, intuitive web app designed to help you track and reflect on your daily moods. Whether you're feeling happy, sad, tired, or stressed, our tool provides gentle suggestions to support your emotional well-being.

Created with care by a team passionate about mental health, Mood Mirror aims to make self-reflection accessible and enjoyable. We believe in the power of small steps towards a happier you.
